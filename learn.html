Our Commitment To Sustainability

How we’re supporting the UN Sustainable Development Goals

Hyper-scale seaweed cultivation has the potential to make a significant contribution to the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals.

02

Zero Hunger

Seaweed production will ensure a sustainable and reliable food source for human consumption as the global population continues to expand.

03

Good health and well-being

Seaweed is a rich source of vitamins and a key component in pharmaceuticals and other health products.

07

Affordable and clean energy

Seaweed as a feedstock for biofuel will contribute to the replacement of fossil fuels.

08

Decent work and economic growth

Rapid growth in the seaweed market will secure employment for millions of people and communities across the globe.

12

Responsible consumption and production

Seaweed products respond to basic human needs and bring a better quality of life, while minimising the use of natural resources, toxic materials, and emissions over their lifecycle.

13

Climate action

Seaweed is critical in addressing the climate challenge: it sequesters greenhouse gases and decreases acidification of the oceans.

14

Life below water

Seaweed beds regenerate marine ecosystems by absorbing pollutants dissolved in the ocean, providing safe habitats and increasing biodiversity.

15

Life on land

Seaweed can play an important role in the development of natural fertilizers that restore nutrients in the soil.
